## 5. Risk Level Definitions Used

| Level   | Definition                                                                                                                  |
| ------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Level 1 | Internal productivity or documentation support. No sensitive data. No customer or user impact.                              |
| Level 2 | Internal workflow support, limited business impact, controlled data use, human review required.                             |
| Level 3 | Customer or user-facing capability, business-critical workflow, sensitive data, compliance exposure.                        |
| Level 4 | Regulated, safety-critical, financial, legal, healthcare, employment, identity, security, or production-impacting use case. |
