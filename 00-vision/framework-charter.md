# Framework Charter

## Purpose

The AI Engineering Crew Framework provides a structured, version-controlled operating
model for business-oriented leaders to direct an AI-native engineering crew. It enables
software solutions to be designed, built, reviewed, deployed, and governed with full
traceability — without requiring the human to become a programmer.

## Scope

Framework Baseline v1.0 covers:

- Human Decision Approver role and accountability
- Five specialist agents: Enterprise Architect, Environment Engineering, Requirement,
  Product Manager, UX/UI
- GitHub and Figma collaboration workspace architecture
- Seven approval gates (Gates 0–6)
- Policies for branching, pull requests, commits, testing, traceability, and decisions
- Templates, reference project, and decision log

Future baselines will add Governance, Builder, Critic, QA, DevOps, and Documentation
agents.

## Audience

- Business sponsors and product owners
- Enterprise architects and engineering leaders
- AI agent operators configuring Gemini Enterprise agents
- Teams adopting AI-assisted software delivery with governance requirements

## Problem Being Solved

Traditional software delivery requires the business leader to either become deeply
technical or delegate entirely to engineering teams with limited visibility. AI agents
can accelerate delivery, but without structure they create chaos: contradictory outputs,
lost decisions, uncontrolled scope, and unapproved builds.

This framework solves the problem by packaging engineering discipline into an AI-native
crew that a strong business leader can direct with confidence.

## Why This Framework Exists

The framework enables a business-oriented human to direct an AI-native engineering crew
without becoming a programmer. It establishes GitHub as the permanent project memory,
Figma as the design workspace, and mandatory human approval gates at every critical
decision point.

## What It Is Not

- This is **not** an application codebase
- This is **not** a replacement for engineering discipline — it packages discipline into
  agents
- This is **not** a prompt library — it is a full operating model with policies and
  gates
- This is **not** autonomous AI development — the human retains final accountability

## Expected Outcome

A professional, version-controlled GitHub repository that serves as the permanent source
of truth for how the AI Engineering Crew operates — enabling repeatable, traceable,
governable software delivery led by a business-oriented Human Decision Approver.
